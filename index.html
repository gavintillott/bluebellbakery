Our story

The Bluebell Ladies

We've always been passionate about baking and loved creating delicious treats for our family and friends. People were always telling us that we should do this for a living — so we took the plunge and started Bluebell Bakery Sussex!


Our ethos is to create delicious, locally sourced treats for everyone to enjoy. We cater for all tastes, from our savoury graze boxes to our sweet bespoke bakes.

Got questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Orders can be placed by contacting us via email, or through our social media pages. We're always happy to chat about what you'd like!
What are your delivery options?
We offer free local collection or free delivery within 5 miles of Chichester. We'll also deliver up to 15 miles from Chichester (delivery charge applies: £5 for 5–10 miles, £10 for 10–15 miles).
When will my order be ready?
Once we receive your order, we'll let you know when it's ready. If you have a specific date in mind, just mention it when you order. During busy periods (e.g. Easter, Christmas) it may take a little longer than usual.
Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?
Yes! Vegan items are highlighted on our menu. Please note we are not a registered vegan kitchen.
How should I store my order?
Everything is freshly baked to order, so it's best enjoyed as soon as possible. Our fudge keeps for up to three weeks in an airtight container. Graze boxes are best consumed on the day of delivery.
Do you make bespoke celebration cakes?
Absolutely! We love making cakes for special occasions. Contact us to discuss your ideas, and we'll talk through design options and pricing.
⚠️ Allergen Information
Whilst we gladly cater for special dietary requirements, we must stress that although every care and effort is taken in the preparation, we cannot guarantee your product will be 100% trace-free of allergens that are carefully stored and used on the premises. These include: wheat flour, barley and rye (cereals containing gluten), eggs, milk, peanuts, nuts (walnuts, ground almonds, and confectionery), soya, sesame seeds, sulphur dioxide and sulphates (dried fruit preservatives). Please contact us if you have any questions.
